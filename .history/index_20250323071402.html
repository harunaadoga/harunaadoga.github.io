Get in Touch

I am a seasoned Network, Systems, and HPC Engineer with a proven track record in designing, automating, and managing large-scale on-premises and cloud infrastructures. I hold a PhD from the University of Glasgow, with a research focus on Network Programmability, Edge/Cloud Computing, and Network Function Virtualization (NFV).

My expertise spans Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using Terraform, automation with Ansible, datacentre networking and server administration, as well as deployment and management of public and private clouds, including OpenStack. I also lead strategic IT planning initiatives, helping organizations align infrastructure with business goals.

I am a published researcher and active member of professional bodies such as the British Computer Society (BCS), IEEE, and the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE). I hold industry certifications including Cisco CCNA/CCNP, Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA), and AWS Solutions Architect Practitioner.

I am passionate about leveraging advanced technologies to solve complex challenges, drive operational efficiency, and deliver innovative infrastructure solutions.
