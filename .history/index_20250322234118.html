About Me

About Me

I am a highly experienced Network, Systems, and HPC Engineer at the University of Sussex, England, with a strong track record in designing, managing, and automating large-scale on-prem and cloud infrastructures. I hold a PhD from the University of Glasgow, specializing in Network Programmability, Edge Computing, Network Function Virtualization (NFV), and High-performance Computing (HPC).

I have published papers on these topics and am a member of several professional organizations, including the British Computer Society (BCS), IEEE, and the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE). I am also certified in networking (Cisco CCNA/CCNP) and cloud computing (AWS).

My passion lies in leveraging cutting-edge technologies to solve complex problems and drive innovation in IT infrastructure and research.
