Professional Profile

I architect and optimize high-performance computing infrastructure for AI and scientific workloads, with a research focus on network programmability and distributed systems.

As an HPC Systems Engineer with a PhD in Computing Science from the University of Glasgow, I specialize in designing, automating, and managing large-scale on-premises and cloud infrastructures. My expertise spans:

  • High-performance networking (200Gbps Ethernet) and storage solutions (Lustre, CEPH)
  • Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible) and cloud platforms (OpenStack, AWS, GCP)
  • Workload managers (Slurm) and container orchestration (Kubernetes)
  • Network programmability, edge computing, and NFV performance optimization

With over 10 years of experience in both industry and academia, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and production-grade infrastructure. I've published in top-tier conferences and journals while delivering robust systems that power scientific discovery and AI innovation.
