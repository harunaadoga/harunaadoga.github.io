About

Who I Am

I am Haruna Adoga. My professional interests centre on Network Programmability, Edge Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Infrastructure Automation, HPC infrastructure supporting diverse workloads including AI and distributed applications, and Cloud Computing.

With a PhD in Computing Science from the University of Glasgow, I bring a research-grounded perspective to practical systems work. My findings have been published in peer-reviewed journals and conferences, and I remain committed to advancing the field through both operational engineering and academic inquiry.
