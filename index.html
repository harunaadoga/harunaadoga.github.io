Who I Am

Hello! I am Haruna Adoga, my professional interests revolve around Network Programmability, Edge Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Infrastructure Automation, HPC infrastructure for handling diverse workloads including AI and distributed applications and Cloud Computing.

With a PhD in Computing Science from the University of Glasgow, UK, I have a strong academic background that complements my practical experience. My research has been published in reputable journals and conferences, and I am passionate about contributing to the advancement of technology through both my professional work and academic pursuits.
